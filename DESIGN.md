# Design World

## World Thesis
A warm, modern, and photo-heavy community hospital identity. It utilizes softened, reassuring colors—alabaster, sage green, and soft coral—combined with large rounded corners and generous photography spaces to build immediate trust and approachability.

## Typography
- **Display**: Fraunces (Variable), used for all headings and major focal points. Retains a human, trustworthy, and dignified character suitable for a community clinic.
- **Body**: Public Sans (Variable), used for readability, UI elements, and scannable text.

## Color System
- health-white (#FCFBF9): Warm alabaster backgrounds, removing harsh clinical glare.
- health-bg (#F2F4F0): Soft tinted grey-green for resting sections.
- health-green (#2C5E48): Primary brand color, a calming sage/emerald.
  - light: #4A8268
  - pale: #E3EDE7 (Accent backgrounds)
- health-red (#D95B5B): Soft coral/rose for primary actions (Testing, Hotlines).
  - dark: #B53B3B
  - light: #ECA2A2
- health-text (#2D3732): Main reading text.
- health-border (#D1DBD5): Subtle dividers.

## Composition & Scale
- Photo-heavy approach with large image placeholders in heroes and cards.
- Deep, soft, diffuse shadows (shadow-card) to give layout a premium, floating feel.
- Friendly, ultra-rounded corners (ounded-3xl and ounded-4xl) replacing sharp edges to convey a cozy, community-focused vibe.

## Signature Elements
- **Divider Ribbon**: A horizontal thin rule (health-border) centered with a subtle health-red pill.
- **Soft UI Cards**: Reassuring card designs featuring large photography at the top, soft shadows, and rounded shapes.
